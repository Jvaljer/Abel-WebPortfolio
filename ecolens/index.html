ECO-LENS
Use time travel to explore & experience the effects of climate change on different environments. See how human actions shape our natural surroundings.
Key Concepts
Through these concepts, this game aims to educate players about the significance of our actions on the global effects of climate change. It highlights how personal waste can affect our environment, encouraging a better understanding of our role in preserving the planet for future generations.

The Game
The game is composed of 3 different environments (Forest, Mountain, Sea), in which different objects are available to manipulations. For each environment, 3 time versions are available: Past, Present, Future.
In each are available 3 objects representing the main wastes from the human society regarding the focused environment.
In the forest player will be able to manipulate a soda bottle, a tire, and a paper-roll.
In the mountain weâ€™ll find objects mainly related to hiking, so a cigarette, a food-wrapper, and an empty drink.
And under the sea we will be able to find an aluminium can, a glass bottle and a trashbag, all representing the wastes threw in the oceans.
Time Travel
Using a Time-Capsule that'll be your ship gathering all the interactions & objects for your journey.
